D12

D12 was a rap supergroup formed in Detroit, Michigan. They existed from 1996 until 2018, when former member Eminem declared it to be "broken up" in his song Stepping Stones.

D12 had four main members by the time it broke up:

  • Kuniva
  • Bizarre
  • Kon Artis
  • Swifty McVay

However, there were two other, extremely influential, members that left before the break-up of the band:

  • Eminem (left 2006)
  • Proof (left 2006)

Devil's Night As stated on Wikipedia: [D12's] debut album, Devil's Night, referring to the tradition of setting unoccupied buildings on fire the night before Halloween, was released in June 2001. It debuted at number one on the U.S. and number two on the UK chart, also reaching the top of the Canadian charts. Devil's Night went on to sell four million albums worldwide and two million in the U.S.
